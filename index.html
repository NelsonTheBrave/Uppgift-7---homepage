Some info about me

Hi! I am student of systems development and web design, focusing mainly on HTML/CSS/JavaScript and Java. My life has gone in many different directions to this point. I was interested in programming already as a kid, when my older sister taught me how to program basic events in Macromedia Flash, and I spent weeks playing around with making games and animated clips. However, I spent most of my adulthood pursuing other interests, such as music, composition and dancing, and ended up doing a bachelor in Environmental Science after a little detour in the field of Psychology. I've lately regained my passion for the creativity and problem solving of programming, and enjoyed feeling like a child once again as I lose track of time tinkering around with functions and flexboxes. It's a privilege to study something and have so much fun at the same time and I look forward to the coming two years of gaining more and more advanced skills and eventually starting my work in this craft.
